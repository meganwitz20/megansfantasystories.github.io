About Megan

Step into a world where mythology, mystery, and transformation weave unforgettable tales. I’m Megan Freitas, an author passionate about crafting emotionally complex narratives that explore loyalty, deception, and resilience.

Currently, I’m working on my fantasy book series, Nycteris Heart—a saga that delves into the hidden truths and celestial balance of Nycteris lore. The first book, Whispers of Nycteris Heart, is already available as an eBook, with the paperback edition coming soon. This is only the beginning; more stories are on the horizon, waiting to be revealed when the time is right.

Beyond writing, I immerse myself in story-driven gaming experiences, where world-building and strategic depth fuel my creativity. I spend my free time exploring the realms of Elder Scrolls Online, Lost Ark, Pax Dei, and Baldur’s Gate 3, each offering inspiration for my own intricate storytelling.

Join me as I bring powerful character journeys and mythology-infused fantasy to life. Whether you’re a fan of epic sagas or intricate character arcs, there’s something here for every fantasy lover.
